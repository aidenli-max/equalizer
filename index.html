60 & 150 Hz are drums and bass . These are "thumps" and "booms."

400 Hz is the lead instruments like guitars , pianos , and vocals. This frequency is what makes it sound "solid."

1k & 2.4k Hz determine how "crunchy" lead instruments sound.

15k Hz are the hats and cymbals . This is the "tst" and "tat."
